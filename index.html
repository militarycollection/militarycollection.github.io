About Us

Welcome to Military Collection! We specialize in sourcing and selling authentic military equipment. Whether you are a passionate historian, collector, or enthusiast, our curated selection offers something for everyone interested in military history. Started in 2012 many vehicles have been used in a number of movie productions such as Gotham, Blind Spot and The Siege. We have a wide range of military vehicles and gear, for rent and for sale.
